Bible Verses

"Let the Word of God be the light that guides your path each day. In every verse, find strength, hope, and the promise of His unfailing love."

📖 Psalm 119:105 — “Your word is a lamp to my feet and a light to my path.”

  • This verse shows how God’s Word gives direction and clarity in life, just like a lamp that lights up a dark path. It reminds the church community that every day, the Bible can guide their decisions and actions.

    • 📖 Philippians 4:6 — “Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God.”

  • This verse comforts us in times of worry. God invites us to turn anxiety into prayer, trusting that He listens and provides.

    • 📖 Jeremiah 29:11 — “‘For I know the plans I have for you,’ declares the Lord, ‘plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future.’”

  • A promise from God assuring us that His plans are always for our good. Even in difficult seasons, He is leading us toward hope and blessings.

    • 📖 Matthew 6:33 — “But seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well.”

  • Jesus teaches us to prioritize God above all. When we put Him first, our daily needs will be taken care of by His faithful hand.

    • 📖 Proverbs 3:5 — “Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding.”

  • We are reminded to rely completely on God rather than our own limited knowledge. True wisdom comes from trusting Him fully.

    • 📖 Isaiah 41:10 — “So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand.”

  • A powerful promise that God is always with us. He gives strength, help, and holds us steady no matter what challenges come.

    • 📖 Nehemiah 8:10 — “Do not grieve, for the joy of the Lord is your strength.”

  • Even in times of sorrow, God’s joy empowers us. It reminds us that strength comes not from circumstances, but from God’s joyful presence.

    • 📖 Philippians 4:13 — “I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.”

  • This verse reminds us that God gives us the strength to face any trial. We are never alone in our struggles because His power works within us.
