UFTCI Cuambogan

Pastor Peter Ruben Collina

Branch: Cuambogan, Tagum City

Years of Service: 6 years

About:Pastor Collina has been a devoted shepherd to the church in Cuambogan for the past 6 years. With unwavering faith and a servant’s heart, he has led the congregation through seasons of growth and transformation. His teachings, deeply rooted in scripture, have strengthened the faith of many and inspired a spirit of unity within the church family. Pastor Collina’s commitment to serving both the church and the wider community continues to make a lasting impact in Cuambogan.
